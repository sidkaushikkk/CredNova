How it Works

1️⃣

Issue

Institutions fill in the details. Metadata is formatted into JSON and uploaded to the IPFS network.

2️⃣

Store

The resulting IPFS hash is immutably anchored to a smart contract on the blockchain network.

3️⃣

Verify

Employers check the blockchain using the Certificate ID to instantly verify authenticity and fetch data.

Key Benefits

⚡

Instant Verification

Eliminate background check delays. Verifiers can check validity in milliseconds without third parties.

🔒

Zero Tampering Risk

Once anchored to the blockchain, a certificate's hash cannot be altered by anyone, including the issuer.

🌍

Decentralized

No single point of failure. Using standard Web3 technologies like IPFS and Polygon/Ethereum.
